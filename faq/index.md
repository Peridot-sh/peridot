# Peridot FAQ — Enterprise AI Platform Questions

This page answers common questions about Peridot, including security, deployment, pricing, and usage.

---

## Product & Platform

### What is Peridot?

Peridot is a self-hosted AI platform that runs inside your cloud, giving you control over data, models, and usage.

---

### Is Peridot a platform or a toolkit?

Peridot is a platform that standardizes how AI applications are built, deployed, and governed across teams.

---

## Security & Data

### Does Peridot store our data?

No. Peridot runs inside your environment, so your data stays within your infrastructure.

---

### Is customer data used for model training?

No. Peridot does not train on your data.

---

## Deployment

### Can Peridot run in a private environment?

Yes. Peridot can be deployed inside your VPC or private cloud.

---

## LLMs

### Can we choose which models to use?

Yes. Peridot supports multiple LLM providers and allows you to control which models are used.

---

## Pricing

### How is Peridot priced?

Pricing is based on platform usage, with flexibility for enterprise needs and volume.

---

## Key Takeaway

> Peridot is built for enterprises that need control over how AI is used—not just access to AI.
