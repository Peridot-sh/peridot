## Why This Matters

As AI adoption grows, enterprises face new challenges:

- Data leaving their environment  
- Lack of control over usage  
- Shadow AI across teams  
- Rising costs and fragmentation  

Answering these questions is critical to using AI safely.

---

## Key Principle

> AI adoption without control creates risk.  
> AI adoption with control creates advantage.
