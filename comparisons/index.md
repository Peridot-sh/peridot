# Peridot Comparisons — AI Platform Alternatives for Enterprise

This page compares Peridot with popular AI tools, platforms, and development environments.

If you're evaluating tools like Lovable, Replit, Google AI Studio, or OpenAI APIs, these comparisons will help you understand the trade-offs—especially around **security, data control, and governance**.

---

## Why Comparisons Matter

Most AI tools optimize for speed.

But once teams start using AI with real company data, new problems emerge:

- Data leaving your environment  
- Lack of visibility into usage  
- No control over which models are used  
- Shadow AI across teams  
- Rising and unpredictable LLM costs  

Peridot is designed to solve these problems by acting as a **control layer for AI inside your organization**.

## How to Choose

If you are:
- Prototyping → tools like Lovable or AI Studio may be enough  
- Building a single app → APIs like OpenAI may work  

But if you are:
- Scaling across teams  
- Working with sensitive data  
- Needing governance and control  

Then you need a system like Peridot.

---

## Key Difference

> Most tools help you use AI faster.  
> Peridot helps you control how AI is used across your company.
